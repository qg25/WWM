# QFYY - Qing Feng Yu Yue 青锋玉月 :full_moon:

## 青锋出鞘映玉月，江湖同心共风流。于刀光剑影之间，我们以义结缘，以剑会友。  
## 青锋玉月，不问出身，不论名利。唯求真心相待，同闯江湖，共绘传奇。  
## 愿每一位行于此道者，皆如青锋般无尘，如玉月般清明。  

**_When the emerald blade shines beneath the jade moon, kindred spirits gather to roam the martial world. Between flashing swords and swirling shadows, we forge bonds of loyalty and friendship._**  
**_Qingfeng Yuyue welcomes the righteous without regard for background or fame. Here we seek only sincerity, venturing together to create our legend._**  
**_May all who walk this path remain spotless as a fresh blade and clear as a moonlit night._**  

## 青 Qing
Symbolizes youth, vitality, and hope; also associated with clarity and ambition, just as the morning sky brings a fresh beginning.  
## 锋 Feng
Represents sharpness, precision, and courage; it describes the keen edge of a blade, standing for determination and the bravery to pursue justice.  
## 玉 Yu
Indicates purity, nobility, and moral beauty; jade is highly valued in Chinese culture for its luster and incorruptibility, symbolizing both physical and spiritual integrity.  
## 月 Yue
Epitomizes serenity, companionship, and dreams; the moon is a timeless emblem of nostalgia, reunion, and the pursuit of light in the darkness.
